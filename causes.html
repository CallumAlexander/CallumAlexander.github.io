    Action for Children

    • Action for Children website
    • How you can help Action for Children
    • Link to 7s donation page

    As a member of Edinburgh University Men's Hockey Club, Action for Children is one of our partner charities that we raise awareness and money for throughout the season. During May 2020, the Club started a fundraiser where each of our seven teams would race each other across the distance between our home pitches, Peffermill, and Lloret de Mar, where the club was meant to have their hockey tour.
    Action for Children work to support disadvantaged young people through difficult and turbulant times in their lives.
