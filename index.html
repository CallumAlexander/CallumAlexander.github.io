Hi, My name is Callum Alexander, I am an incoming 3rd year undergraduate studying Informatics (Computer Science) at the University of Edinburgh. I have academic interests in data science, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.

I am the Website & Publicity Secretary for Edinburgh University Men's Hockey Club, the most successful university men's hockey club in Scotland, and the largest university hockey club in the United Kingdom. My role is to manage the image of the club and perform outreach, via our website and social media pages. I headed the first major update to the Club's website in 7 years. Click here to see the site.

From January til June 2021, I was a Quality Assurance Engineer at HYPED, where I helped develop a testing framework for the 16,000+ line C++ codebase.

When I manage to free up some time from studying and working, I play hockey for Edinburgh University Men's Hockey Club, read Brian Herbert and other authors, and nunchucks, and write scripts to display Cowsay quoting Sacha Baron Cohen.
Recently, I have switched from Fedora 43 to Ubuntu LTS, and begun 'ricing' my OS. My workspace is in a constantly changing cycle between GNOME and Qtile WM.
I am an Asura Ranger found exploring on Fisher of Woe, a very budget FIFA player, and a half-decent Mario Kart Wii player with Funky Kong and the Bowser Bike.
